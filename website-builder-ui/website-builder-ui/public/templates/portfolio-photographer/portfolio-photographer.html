About Nia

Born in Lagos, raised between two continents — Nia's work is defined by an uncompromising commitment to truth. She spent a decade documenting communities in West Africa, Southeast Asia, and South America for international NGOs and press agencies before turning her lens to fine art.

Her debut monograph Faces of Lagos (Aperture, 2024) sold out its first print run of 5,000 copies within a week.
