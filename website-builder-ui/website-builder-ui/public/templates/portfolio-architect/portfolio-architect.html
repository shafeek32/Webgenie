Design Philosophy

I

Contextual Sensitivity

Architecture that listens to its site — its history, culture, climate, and the lives of people who will inhabit it.

II

Material Honesty

Materials expressed as they are: concrete, timber, steel — neither disguised nor over-decorated.

III

Sustainable by Default

Every project targets carbon-zero or carbon-negative performance. Sustainability is not optional.

IV

Time as Material

Buildings that age gracefully, that improve with patina, that earn their place in the city decade by decade.
